Account & Data Deletion

Ansari Traders Grocery

Last updated: 4 January 2026

This page explains how users of the Ansari Traders Grocery mobile application can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to request account deletion

  1. Open the email account that you used to sign in to the app (your Google account).
  2. Send an email to alansariapk2025@gmail.com with the subject line: “Delete my Ansari Traders Grocery account”.
  3. In the body of the email, include the following information:
    • Your full name
    • The Google email address you use in the app
    • Your phone number (optional, for verification)
  4. After we verify your request, we will delete or anonymize your account and personal data as described below, normally within 30 days.

What data will be deleted

When you request account deletion, we will delete or anonymize, where applicable:

  • Your account profile (name, email address, phone number).
  • Your saved delivery addresses and preferences.
  • Authentication tokens and active sessions used to sign in to the app.

What data may be retained

We may retain certain information where we are legally required to do so or where we have a legitimate business reason, for example:

  • Order and transaction records that we must keep for accounting, tax, and fraud‑prevention purposes.
  • Server logs and backup copies that will be deleted automatically after their normal retention period.

Any retained information is limited to what is necessary and is no longer used to provide you with app services once your account has been deleted.

After your account is deleted

After we complete deletion of your account:

  • You will no longer be able to sign in with your previous account.
  • You may still install and browse the app as a guest. If you sign in again with Google, a new account will be created.

More information

For full details about how we collect, use, store, and protect your data, please see our Privacy Policy .

If you have any questions about this page or about a deletion request, please contact us at alansariapk2025@gmail.com.
